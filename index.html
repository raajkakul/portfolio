Hey there! I'm Shresha Das, a dynamic full stack developer and web enthusiast currently navigating Machine Learning in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. Anticipating graduation in December 2024 with a Master's degree, my academic journey covers a broad spectrum, including algorithm design, machine learning, computer vision, web development, cognitive science, human computer interaction, and more.

As an SDE Intern at Amazon Web Services, I crafted auto diagnostic tools, streamlined debugging, and built a nifty Linux CLI using Boto3. Passionate about music and culture, I also was an Executive Board Member for Aarohi and India club at Georgia Tech, orchestrating successful virtual concerts and events on campus. From leading tech projects to harmonizing melodies, I bring creativity and hard work to every endeavor. Let's embark on this journey together!
